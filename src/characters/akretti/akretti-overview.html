Character Sheet

Akretti

A kindly elderly alchemist, who weaves enchantment through the delicate balance of curiosity, perseverance, and empathy. With a charming stall filled with potions, each a testament to their uncanny ability to understand the alchemical dance of ingredients, Akretti fosters connections with customers, offering not just remedies but solace and hope. Their journey of self-discovery, marked by colorful stains and missing finger, is a testament to a lifetime dedicated to the alchemical art.
